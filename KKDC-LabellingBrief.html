05

Key Rules to Remember

🤖
Don't re-label what AI already labelled. If 1.QUOTE, 3.SLS, and 5.NY are already there — leave them. AI runs every 5 minutes on new emails.
✅
2.DONE = CRM entered. Only apply 2.DONE when you have fully entered the job into CRM/DocuGen. This is the signal that the email has served its purpose.
🔄
Only one status label at a time. Remove the old status before adding the new one. e.g. remove 2.03 awaiting-client before adding 2.04 awaiting-factory.
🚫
Never delete emails. The label system replaces the need to delete. If an email is done, apply 2.DONE and leave it.
⚡
Urgent? Add 2.11 urgent in addition to any other status label. This will surface it in your Multi-inbox urgent view immediately.
📋
Technical requirements. Add 4.Submittal or 4.WD.Dwg as soon as you identify the requirement — don't wait until it becomes a problem.
